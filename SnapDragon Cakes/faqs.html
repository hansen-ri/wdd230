Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does your pricing work?

Cake pricing depends on the size of the cake and the amount of decorating involved with your cake.

How much notice do I need to give for a custom cake?

At least a week's notice is preferable.

What flavors do you offer?

We can provide you a list of cake possibilities and recommendations.

Do you deliver?

Delivery is an option, and when possible an additional fee will be added that appropriately reflects the distance of the delivery.

Do you require a deposit?

For larger/complex cakes a deposit may be required.
